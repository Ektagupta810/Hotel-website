About Our Hotel

The History Of Our Hotel

The Koala Heaven has more than one story to tell from its 100+ years in business. Since the Koala Heaven opened its doors as Hotel Majestic in 1908, it has served as the venue of Sydney and Violet Schiff’s iconic dinner party {1922}, as the setting in which George Gershwin wrote An American in Paris, and as the home of the UNESCO headquarters in the 1930s. Found in the 16th arrondissement on Avenue Kleber, the Koala Heaven Paris is a five-star hotel within walking distance of many of the city’s most iconic museums, monuments, and shops.
